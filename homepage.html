About Gajraj

Gajraj Group has been"Constructing Dreams" since 1998 and is involved in Real Estate, Import & Export and other business, for more than a decade now. Over this year our mission has been to turn vacant land into bustlings hubs, not just to create concrete structures but to be a medium of realization if cherished dreams of million of lives. Our mission, vision and motto is just one to create spaces that customers are proud to own.

We have been involved in Residential and Commercial developments all over kolkata. The company is acknowledged for quality, commitments, integrity and systematic execution of projects, not only by buyers, business partners but also by peers. Growth, expansion and diversification which are the prime factors determining the success of any company, we have not remained untouched by them and we are positively looking forward for the expansion, both in current fields as well as new projects.
